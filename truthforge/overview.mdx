## What is TruthForge?

TruthForge is a **multi-agent AI verification platform** built on the Hedera blockchain. It solves one of global trade's biggest problems: **you can't trust the documents and photos that come with a shipment**.

Think of it like a team of 5 specialized AI inspectors that automatically check every shipment before it arrives at port — detecting fake photos, verifying shipping documents, and writing tamper-proof receipts on the blockchain.

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="The Problem" icon="triangle-exclamation" color="#ef4444">
    Ports lose billions annually to document fraud, fake cargo photos, and manual clearance delays that take days instead of minutes.
  </Card>
  <Card title="The Solution" icon="circle-check" color="#22c55e">
    5 AI agents working together to verify shipments in minutes, with every result permanently recorded on Hedera's blockchain.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## The Core Value Proposition

| Problem | TruthForge Solution |
|:--------|:--------------------|
| Port clearance takes days | Autonomous pre-arrival verification in minutes |
| Document fraud is rampant | Immutable, blockchain-anchored cryptographic proof |
| Data is siloed across carriers | Unified multi-agent network on Hedera HOL |
| Compliance is manual | 5 autonomous agents manage the entire workflow |
